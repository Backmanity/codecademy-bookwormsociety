Book of the Month

Margaret Atwood: The Handmaid's tale

The Handmaid's Tale is a futuristic dystopian novel published in 1985. It is set in a near-future New England in a patriarchal, totalitarian theonomic state known as the Republic of Gilead, which has overthrown the United States government. Offred is the central character and narrator and one of the "handmaids", women who are forcibly assigned to produce children for the "commanders", who are the ruling class in Gilead.
